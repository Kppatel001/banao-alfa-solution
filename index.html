Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about ChanloPay.

ChanloPay allows you to create a digital event, generate a unique UPI QR code and payment link. Guests can then scan the QR or click the link to contribute money directly to your linked UPI ID using their preferred UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM, Paytm, etc.). You can track all contributions in real time on your dashboard.

No, ChanloPay does not store your money. All payments are directly transferred from your guest's UPI app to your linked UPI bank account. ChanloPay only facilitates the digital collection and provides tracking and management tools.

ChanloPay supports all major UPI applications, including Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM, Paytm, and any other app that supports UPI payments. Guests can choose their preferred app to make a contribution.

Guests can pay in two ways: 1) They can scan the unique ChanloPay QR code generated for your event using any UPI app. 2) You can share the payment link via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Clicking the link will open their preferred UPI app for direct payment.

Yes, depending on your chosen plan, you can export detailed payment reports in PDF and Excel formats. This helps you with record keeping and thank-you notes.

Absolutely. ChanloPay uses Google Authentication for secure logins and is built on a robust Firebase backend with enterprise-grade security rules. All data is encrypted, and we adhere to strict data protection protocols.

For plans that include WhatsApp receipts, once a guest makes a payment, a confirmation message and receipt can be automatically sent to them (if their number is linked or provided during payment) and to you, confirming the contribution.
